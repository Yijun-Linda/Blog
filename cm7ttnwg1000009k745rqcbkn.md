# [China Shanghai International Arts Festival](https://www.artsbird.com/en)

China Shanghai International Arts Festival (CSIAF) is a state-level comprehensive international arts festival hosted by the Ministry of Culture and Tourism of the People’s Republic of China and organized by the Shanghai Municipal People’s Government.

## Project Coordinator Intership

* **Facilitated stakeholder involvement and alignment:** Acted as the central liaison for 15 diverse art projects, mapping out the distinct needs and expectations of artists and organizers to ensure successful exhibition delivery.
    
* **Developed engagement tools:** Conceptualized and led the development of a "reservation mini-program" to streamline visitor interaction. This digital tool bridged the gap between operational requirements and visitor needs, significantly enhancing the participatory experience.
    
* **Protocol execution:** Managed the "Zhang Garden Project" by breaking down complex goals into executable tasks, establishing clear timelines, and mediating between vendors and internal teams to resolve emerging issues.
    
* **Curatorial support:** Conducted material reviews for 50 shortlisted projects, filtering and selecting content that aligned with the festival’s broader strategic themes.
    

### 21st Artsbird 2019

ART SPACE The “Art Space” event uses the city as the stage backdrop, creating a “theater without walls”, and a “15-minute art & culture circle” at the doorsteps of people. The event gathers nearly 2,000 artists from over 30 countries and regions, presenting over 90 performances in 30 indoor and outdoor venues around the city. Furthermore, this event also explores the cultural highlights of each city district, tied closely with the themes of “Belt and Road,” “Digital Vitality,”creating “one district, one highlight”, allowing art to mix into citizens’ lives, becoming a shining city scenery.

# 中国上海国际艺术节 - 文旅局下属机构 | 项目协调实习生 | 2019年

在2019年，我有幸担任中国上海国际艺术节的项目协调实习生，参与了演展部的艺术空间系列活动。在这5个月的实习期间，我不仅深入了解了艺术节的运作机制，还积累了宝贵的项目管理经验。

### 材料审核与评选支持

在这个项目中，我负责审核50个入围项目的材料，并支持评选的先期沟通工作。这一过程让我体验到项目协调的重要性，尤其是在确保每个项目都符合艺术节的标准和要求方面。

### 预算协调

与带教经理一起，我参与了15个项目的预算和演出呈现问题的协调。这一经历让我明白，精细的预算管理和有效的沟通是成功项目实施的关键。

### 大型活动的策划与执行

在大型活动的策划与执行中，我深刻体会到需要将复杂的课题拆解至可执行的颗粒度，同时梳理时间节点。此外，我还锻炼了应对现场突发状况的能力，这在活动当天尤为重要。

### 项目协调与现场管理

我负责协调多种演出和展览活动，与国际合作伙伴保持沟通，并监督超过100个项目和表演的执行。为了提高观众入场的效率，我实施了一款预约移动应用，极大地改善了票务管理。

### 创意与设计

在创意方面，我展现了在ART SPACE宣传册的构思与设计上的能力。这一作品不仅展示了活动的独特性，还吸引了众多观众的关注。
